Finding Friendship and Happiness in University Life

Early Friendships and Initial Struggles

When I joined the university, I first made friends with Ibtasam and then Huzaifa. Their presence made things a little easier for me in the beginning. However, I faced a problem during my hostel room allotment. Even though I made the payment, it failed, and because of this issue, I was not given a proper room. Instead, I was placed in a hall, which was not what I expected and made me feel disappointed.

Patience, Acceptance, and Adjustment

At that moment, I had no choice but to accept the situation. I had to trust my fate and practice patience. Living in a hall was uncomfortable at first, and it felt like things were not going my way. But instead of complaining, I decided to adjust and stay patient. This phase taught me an important lesson—that sometimes life does not go as planned, and we must stay calm and hopeful.

Unexpected Happiness and Strong Friendships

With time, things started to change in a positive way. In those same halls, I found some of the best friends of my life. The place that once felt uncomfortable slowly became enjoyable and full of good memories. I began to feel happy and grateful for everything. Now, I truly enjoy my university and hostel life, realizing that sometimes the best things come from unexpected situations.
